## Challenge

The ask from PM was "what if we took another look at Adobe Color?" We love ambiguity. I took this from a vague ask to tangible value by scoping and leveraging user research, developing POCs, proving business value and selling this up through product SVP. I pivoted from internal alignment, over to driving research, design, and dev requirements—creating a more usable, functional, and accessible experience for more than 15mil annual users visiting the previously abandoned Adobe Color experience.

## Results

End to end redesign with improved functionality that ensure the user palette follows them across extract, palette generator, and accessibility features. Designed usable mobile experiences for each product with clear flows into CC library save and product. Completed comprehensive accessibility bluelines that ensure flows are intuitive for user regardless of vision, keyboard, or assistive technology usage.
