## Challenge

Enter a high performance, fast moving team fresh out of a rebrand with a clunky WYSIWYG editor on top of Wordpress. During my time at Thimble, I drove growth focused optimizations and full page redesigns on the logged out surface as well as pioneering a design and motion system across marketing and product. I spent my days here building a comprehensive design ecosystem from Figma to code, collaborating with brand, growth, marketing, lifecyle, and product teams to build short and long term growth pipelines for the business.

## Results

Results
Designed and collaborated with engineers on an MVP design system, including a system of interaction animations, and moved the site from Elementor to ACF. This decreased go-live time across the project and reduced QA time and bloat on the site. After going live, we benefitted from a <strong>32% increase</strong> in performance, <strong>1.5 second decrease</strong> in time to LCP (largest contentful paint) and a <strong>reduction to 0 seconds total blocking time</strong>.

Once the design system was in place, I continued to test full page redesigns and optimiziations. I solved for some core user experience issues and designed a new product page template, including adding the ability for a user to get a quote on page for their policy. This test saw a <strong>13% increase in CTA clicks</strong> and a <strong>2% increase in transactions</strong>.

A homepage redesign focused on improved brand expression and saw a <strong>10% increase in CTA clicks</strong>, <strong>3.4% increase in transactions</strong> and a <strong>7.8% decrease in bounce rate</strong>.

We also ran smaller optimizations that leveraged personalization for improved return to quote experiences. Our initial test ran over a few weeks and resulted in <strong>1,353 more new leads</strong> and <strong>6.5% increase in conversion (policies bound)</strong>.
